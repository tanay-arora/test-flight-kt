When is the best time to travel to Orlando?

Orlando is usually best visited between March and May. Expect pleasant weather throughout this period, with an average temperature of about 28°C.

Consider visiting in the winter, from December to February, if you'd rather avoid the masses or want to keep some money. Just be conscious that although it's normally pleasant, with highs of 22°C. There may be rare fronts of cold that bring with them lower temps.
