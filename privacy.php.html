We Gather and Utilize Data

We gather "personal details" that may be used to get in touch with you or put you in contact with someone else. This Policy does not distinguish between "Personal information" and "Personal data."

The following data is directly gathered by Cheapatravel.com from its customers:

Personal information such as your name, birthday, gender, age, & so on.

Provide your phone number, email address, and mailing address in your contact information.

The reservation contains the name of the travelers, frequent flyer number, passport information, reservation number, arrival and departure dates and times other details of Flight.

Account data such as login Information, such as email address, and password.

How are your data stored and protected?

Travelowonder keeps all security measures up to date, including organizational, technological, and physical defenses, to keep your information safe from theft, misuse, loss, and destruction.

When communicated over our services, your payment information will be protected by many cryptographic mechanisms. To be even more precise, we do not retain your payment information unless you specifically request that we do so. We forward your payment details to the relevant parties when you request a flight reservation so that the transaction can be finalized. Travel agents that receive your payment data are required by our agreements to maintain the security, protection, and confidentiality of that information.

How are we going to share your personal data?

In order to assist you with booking your trip with us, to help with your trip and/or vacation, to stay in compliance with the law, and to contact with you, your personal information may be shared. How we receive confidential data is explained in full in the Privacy Statement.
